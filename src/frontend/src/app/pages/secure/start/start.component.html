Welcome to ingoodhands

Welcome to ingoodhands, your go-to platform for delivering aid to those in need.

As a user, you will find the menu with all the application's pages located on the left side of the screen. Our mission, as creators of this platform, is to spread kindness and charity by connecting donors with those who require essential supplies such as food, drink, and defensive equipment. The current war in Ukraine has made it imperative for Western countries to come together and offer their support.

Whether you're a Donor, who wants to provide gifts through the web application, a Recipient who needs to place an order for specific items, a Warehouse Manager who takes care of storing and preparing the deliveries, or a Delivery Person who transports the packages, your role in this process is crucial. We believe that by working together, we can make a positive impact and bring hope to those in need.

Join us in our mission to bring a brighter future to Ukraine. Thank you for being a part of ingoodhands.
